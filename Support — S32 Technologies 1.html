24/7 Global Client Support

We deeply value your trust in S32 Technologies as your cybersecurity service provider. Our commitment to your satisfaction is paramount, which is why we offer a dedicated account manager who is available 24/7 to address any inquiries or support needs you may have. Your account manager will collaborate with specialized support and engineering teams to ensure your requests are promptly and accurately addressed. 

In the event your account manager is unavailable, your call will be seamlessly directed to our 24/7 Global Client Support Center. You can also opt to leave a message for your account manager by pressing 1, bypassing the support center.

Cyber Incident Response Team

S32 Technologies offers comprehensive Cyber Incident Response services to swiftly address critical cybersecurity incidents encountered by our esteemed clients. Cyber incidents, such as network breaches, data leaks, ransomware attacks, and other security breaches, pose significant threats to organizational integrity and data security. Our Cyber Incident Response Team (CIRT) stands ready to mitigate these risks effectively and efficiently. Clients can report cyber incidents seamlessly through our all-in-one client portal. Upon logging in, they can navigate to the support section and open a ticket under the cyber incident category, marking it as urgent. Our dedicated Cyber Incident Response Team commits to responding within 15 minutes or less to the ticket submitter, ensuring rapid resolution and minimal disruption to operations. This method offers clients a faster and more convenient avenue for reporting incidents.

Secure Client Portal

In the event clients are unable to access the client portal due to a cyber incident or you prefer direct assistance, please contact the CIRT Cyber Command Center Hotline for immediate assistance from our CIRT team. 

Your trust in S32 Technologies is our utmost priority, and we stand ready to assist you in safeguarding your digital assets against emerging threats.
